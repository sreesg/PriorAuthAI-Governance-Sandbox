# Rules: POL-ONC-220 — Oncology Imaging: PET/CT Scan

## Policy: POL-ONC-220 (Oncology Imaging: PET/CT Scan)
- CPT Code: 78816, 78815, 78814
- Allowed ICD-10 Diagnosis Codes: [Not Specified]
- CRIT-1: Confirmed or strongly suspected malignancy with pathology report or imaging findings.
- CRIT-2: Clinical indication is initial staging, restaging after treatment, or evaluation of suspected recurrence.
- CRIT-3: Conventional imaging (CT or MRI) must have been performed first and found insufficient for clinical decision-making.
- CRIT-4: Requesting physician must be an oncologist, pulmonologist, or surgeon managing the malignancy.
- CRIT-5: PET/CT scan must documented to change the treatment plan (e.g., determine surgical candidacy, radiation field planning).
