# Rules: POL-RX-880 — Specialty Pharmacy: Dupixent (dupilumab) for Atopic Dermatitis

## Policy: POL-RX-880 (Specialty Pharmacy: Dupixent (dupilumab) for Atopic Dermatitis)
- CPT Code: J0875
- Allowed ICD-10 Diagnosis Codes: [L20.x]
- CRIT-1: Confirmed diagnosis of moderate-to-severe atopic dermatitis (IGA >= 3 OR EASI >= 16 OR BSA >= 10%)
- CRIT-2: Failed or contraindicated to at least one topical corticosteroid (medium/high potency) for $\ge$ 4 weeks.
- CRIT-3: Failed or contraindicated to at least one topical calcineurin inhibitor (tacrolimus or pimecrolimus) for $\ge$ 4 weeks.
- CRIT-4: Failed, intolerant, or contraindicated to at least one systemic immunosuppressant (methotrexate, cyclosporine, azathioprine, or mycophenolate) for $\ge$ 3 months.
- CRIT-5: Prescribing physician must be a dermatologist or allergist/immunologist.
- CRIT-6: Patient age $\ge$ 6 years.
- CRIT-7: No active systemic infection and TB screening completed.
