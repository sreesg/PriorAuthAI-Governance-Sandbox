# Rules: POL-RAD-501 — Advanced Imaging: Lumbar Spine MRI

## Policy: POL-RAD-501 (Advanced Imaging: Lumbar Spine MRI)
- CPT Code: 72148, 72149, 72158
- Allowed ICD-10 Diagnosis Codes: [Not Specified]
- CRIT-1: Conservative therapy for minimum 6 weeks with documented failure
- CRIT-2: Neurological symptoms present (radiculopathy, numbness/tingling, weakness, or bowel/bladder dysfunction)
- CRIT-3: Red flag symptoms absent OR urgent/emergent pathway if present
- CRIT-4: No MRI of the same region within the past 12 months unless new symptoms documented
