Agentic AI for Healthcare Governance

Autonomous Prior Authorization
with Dual-Agent Quality Assurance

A governed AI system where two independent agents — a PA Review Agent and a Challenger Agent — work together to deliver fast, accurate, and fully auditable clinical authorization decisions.

End-to-end PA agentic architecture: Skills, Rules, Hooks, Progressive Disclosure, ClinicalNLP Engine, and Challenger Agent

Core Capabilities

🤖

PA Review Agent

Evaluates clinical notes against payer policy criteria using skills, rules, and hooks. Routes to approve or escalate — never auto-denies.

🔁

Challenger Agent

Autonomous second opinion that stress-tests every decision. Catches temporal inconsistencies, credential gaps, and ambiguous evidence.

🧠

ClinicalNLP Engine

Local LLM (Gemma 4 12B) for semantic extraction from clinical notes. Handles negation, paraphrases, and implicit clinical references.

🔒

Progressive Disclosure

Agent receives context incrementally at each pipeline stage — preventing overload and enforcing separation of concerns architecturally.

📄

Multi-Policy System

Four policy domains (Radiology, Oncology, Surgery, Specialty Rx) with dynamic routing based on CPT codes. PDF-sourced criteria.

🧬

Full Audit Trail

Every skill invocation, rule evaluation, and hook firing is traced with timestamps. Complete transparency from intake to decision.
